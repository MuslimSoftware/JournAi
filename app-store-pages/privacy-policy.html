JournAi Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 22, 2026

JournAi is a local-first journaling app. This policy explains what information is processed when you use JournAi and how that information is handled.

1. Information You Create in the App

JournAi stores the content you create in the app, including journal entries, todos, sticky notes, and app preferences. This data is stored locally on your device.

2. Optional AI Features

If you enable AI features and provide your own OpenAI API key, selected app content is sent to OpenAI to generate chat responses, summaries, embeddings, and insights that you request.

OpenAI is an independent service provider, and its processing is governed by OpenAI's terms and privacy policy.

3. API Key Handling

When provided, your OpenAI API key is stored using platform secure storage (for example, iOS Keychain) and is not sold to third parties.

4. No Account Required

JournAi does not require account creation to use core functionality. The app is designed to operate offline for journaling features.

5. Data Sharing

  • JournAi does not sell your personal data.
  • JournAi does not use third-party advertising SDKs.
  • JournAi does not share your journal content except when you explicitly use AI features.

6. Security

JournAi uses local encryption and app-level protections to help protect your data on device. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we use reasonable safeguards appropriate to the app.

7. Your Choices

  • You can use JournAi without enabling AI features.
  • You can export your data from the app at any time.
  • You can delete local app data by removing content or uninstalling the app.

8. Children's Privacy

JournAi is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact me@younesbenketira.com.
